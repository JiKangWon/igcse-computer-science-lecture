Computer Science

IGCSE Syllabus

Lecturer: Nguyen Chi Cuong

Syllabus

Chapter 1: Data Representation Binary, hexadecimal, sound, images, and compression. Chapter 2: Data Transmission Serial, parallel, packet switching, and error detection. Chapter 3: Computer Hardware CPU, Von Neumann, I/O devices, and storage. Chapter 4: Software Operating systems, utilities, translators, and interrupts. Chapter 5: The Internet and Its Uses WWW, HTTP, DNS, cyber security, and blockchain. Chapter 6: Automated & Emerging Tech Automation, robotics, and Artificial Intelligence (AI). Chapter 7: Algorithm Design Problem-solving, pseudocode, flowcharts, and testing. Chapter 8: Programming Variables, loops, arrays, procedures, and file handling. Chapter 9: Databases Tables, records, fields, primary keys, and SQL. Chapter 10: Boolean Logic Logic gates (AND, OR, NOT, XOR), truth tables, and logic circuits. Chapter 11: 1D Arrays (Pseudo Code) Declaring, traversing, summing, and finding max in 1D arrays. Chapter 12: 2D Arrays (Pseudo Code) Using nested loops, matrix traversal, and row sums.
